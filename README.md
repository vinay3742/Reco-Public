# Reco: User Guide & Feature Overview
**Your Privacy-First, Secure Biometric Authentication Assistant.**

Welcome to **Reco**, a modern Android application designed for secure, high-speed biometric registration and recognition. Unlike traditional systems, Reco processes everything directly on your device, ensuring your data never leaves your hands.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Secure Face Registration
Quickly onboard new users with our intelligent registration system. The app guides you through the process to ensure a high-quality "Face Profile" is created for maximum accuracy.

### 2. Instant Face Recognition
Identify registered individuals in a couple of seconds. Perfect for attendance tracking, secure 
access, or personalized experiences.

### 3. Comprehensive Profile Management
View and manage registered identities, update their information, or review their attendance history.

### 4. Enterprise-Ready Reports
Export your registration and attendance data into professionally formatted **Excel (.xlsx)** files, ready for management or HR systems.

### 5. Privacy-First Design
- **100% On-Device**: All facial processing happens on your phone. No data is sent to the cloud.
- **Biometric Security**: Your facial data is converted into a mathematical code that cannot be turned back into a photo.
- **Encrypted Backups**: If you choose to back up your data, it is protected with AES-256 encryption.

---

## How to Use Reco

### 1. Registering a New User
To ensure the best recognition speed and accuracy, follow these steps during registration:
1. **Find Good Lighting**: Ensure the person's face is well-lit and not in deep shadow.
2. **Follow the Prompts**: The app might ask you to "Move Closer" or "Center Your Face."
3. **Hold Still**: The app takes multiple samples to create a robust profile.
4. **Enter Details**: Provide the name, Employee ID, and a secure passcode to finish.

### 2. Recognizing a User
1. Navigate to the **Recognition** screen.
2. Point the camera at the person's face.
3. The system will instantly display the user's name and ID once matched.

### 3. Exporting Data
1. Go to the **Settings** or **Reports** section.
2. Select the data you wish to export.
3. Tap **Export to Excel**. The file will be saved to your device's downloads.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Avoid Extremes**: Recognition works best when the face is at a natural angle (looking toward the camera).
- **Lighting**: Avoid strong backlight (like standing in front of a bright window).


## Troubleshooting

- **"Face Not Detected"**: Ensure there are no obstructions (like masks or very dark sunglasses) and that you are in a well-lit area.
- **"Move Closer"**: The face is too small in the frame. Move the camera slightly closer to the subject.
- **Recognition Failed**: If you've significantly changed your appearance (e.g., grew a full beard), we recommend updating your profile in the Registration section.
